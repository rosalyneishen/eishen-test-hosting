Nestled squarely between Nacogdoches and Lufkin Texas, the Redland Drive-In RV Park is conveniently located on US Highway 59, soon to be the Interstate 69 thoroughfare.

Built on the site of the historical Redland Drive-In Movie Theater, the park offers great amenities at a great price to its guests and residents. Featuring free High Speed Internet, a super-efficient Wi-Fi network, a commercial laundry room, and a relaxing game room.

Check us out! Tour the Park and meet our staff. Our single goal is to make you feel at home and comfortable.
